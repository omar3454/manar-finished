ABOUT AL MANAR GROUP

Al Manar group was established in 1975 as an import company, and today Al Manar group stands proud as a leading, experienced, and constantly growing company with a wide range of activities including import , export , storage , manufacturing and distribution. Our objective is to keep our various clients satisfied to constantly furnish the continuously growing local and international market with the best in class products; assuring that the range of products we are presenting is of superior quality, genuine, and compatible with the local and international markets.
