ELF Products

Performance (PERFO)

Perfo HDX 700 (15W-40)

ELF PERFO HDX 700 (15W-40) is recommended for naturally aspirated or turbocharged diesel engines of commercial vehicles (trucks, buses, vans, and utility vehicles), off-road vehicles, construction equipment operating in all severe conditions.

Perfo HDX 500 (20W-50)

ELF PERFO HDX 500 (20W-50) is recommended for naturally aspirated or turbocharged diesel engines of commercial vehicles (trucks, buses, vans, and utility vehicles), off-road vehicles, construction equipment operating in all severe conditions.

Perfo HDX 100 [SAE/CD]

Single grade oil for gasoline and diesel engines. Fits high mileage vehicles. Every use case (street, road, highways). Designed for certain vehicles and/or utility vehicles before 1998. International standards API SF/CD.

Perfo HDX 200 [SAE-50]

Recommended for all turbocharged or normally aspirated diesel engines of commercial vehicles. Suitable for all stationary diesel engines for gearboxes and hydraulic systems when the manufacturer requires a motor oil of a suitable grade for these applications.

EVOLUTION

EVOLUTION-300 [20W-50]

Mineral technology oil designed for older gasoline and diesel passenger car engines. Suitable for use under standard conditions (in the city, on highways or motorways). Meets automotive manufacturers’ needs for extended oil change intervals.

EVOLUTION-400 [20W-50]

These oils can be used with gasoline or diesel engine technologies without particulate filters and engines manufactured before 2001 and designed for use with mineral oils of these viscosities. Suitable for all conditions. Meets automotive manufacturers’ needs for extended oil change intervals.

EVOLUTION-900 FT

Elf High Performance Synthetic Engine Oil is intended for gasoline engines, especially newer ones. Suitable for all journeys, especially in extreme conditions, and in all driving styles, especially high and high speeds.

Other ELF Lubricants

FRELUB 450

SSynthetic brake fluid is recommended for brake and clutch systems in all vehicles that use a synthetic fluid where a DOT 4 standard is required. Viscosity is suitable for low and high temperatures. This fluid is suitable for high temperature.

GEAR ELF

High viscosity index, allowing sufficient fluidity at low temperature to maintain gearbox functionality in cold running conditions, while maintaining sufficient oil film thickness for optimal bearing protection using advanced technology and the best automotive fluid specifications on the market.

ELFOLNA

High performance anti-wear hydraulic oils for construction machinery. High protection against corrosion ensuring maximum life. Superior thermal stability to avoid sludge formation at high temperature.

ELFMATIC DII

Lubricant for automatic gearboxes and power steering of all light vehicles. The viscosity index is very high. Excellent thermal and chemical stability. Excellent abrasion resistance and anti-corrosion.
