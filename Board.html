Abdel-Alim Nawara

Chairman of Al Manar Group

With over 40 years in management, economics, and international business, Mr. Abdel Alim has been President of the Egyptian-Tunisian Economic Chamber, Chairman of the Cairo Chamber of Commerce, and Board Member of the French Arab Chamber of Commerce in Paris.

Ahmed Nawara

CEO of Al Manar Group

Ahmed, a School of Business graduate with an Executive Master’s from AUC and a PhD from the Paris School of Business, led Al Manar’s North African expansion. He secured contracts with Honeywell and Total, driving significant growth and establishing Al Manar as a market leader.

MOhamed Nawara

CSO of Al Manar Group

Mohamed, an Economics graduate from AUC, joined Al Manar in 1997 as a fresh graduate. After 15 years in IT, founding startups in e-commerce and IT trade, he rejoined Al Manar in 2014, focusing on support functions and business development.

ALY ABO SAIF​
