Our Workplace Values.

Al-Manar Group has excellent opportunities to welcome talented employees into a vibrant workplace.

Our values are based on the following principles, which we call the CCTL pillars.

Communication

Talk Speak up, respect your differences, make meaningful comments, and communicate honestly and openly.

Cooperation

Participation – working constructively together to achieve a common goal. Team support, participation always brings the best solutions.

Learning

Encourage continuous learning and openness to new ideas. Learn from both successes and failures, and seek opportunities for improvement. Share knowledge and promote a culture of learning to build a stronger, more adaptable team.

Trust

Trust it – be open in front of and with others. Act with integrity and respect, inspire positive expectations, and communicate honestly.
